About Us

PocketSense is designed and targeted for the usage of children so that they can manage their pocket money, align it with goals that they want to achieve financially. This will help them inculcate the habit of financial planning, which is a critical skill that is needed by an individual as they grow up. Be it an engineer, management graduate or any one from any walk of life, they will have to financially plan their expenditure after a certain age. We, via PocketSense, we want to equip and instill these essential life skills into the minds of the individuals of tomorrow: the kids. PocketSense helps the child to track the money he spent, the savings he needs to do and the amount that he needs to keep for contingencies. The parent will fix a monthly allowance for the child and the application will advise as to how much money he/she should ideally save in order to achieve his/her goal. The child can also choose the time frame within which the goal is intended to be completed.

The problem it targets is lack of financial planning and monitoring funds on an individual level. Via PocketSense we want to equip and instill these essential life skills into the minds of the individuals of the future. Regardless of whichever profession a person follows, managing personal finance is an essential skill and requirement. This is a great way for parents to make their child financially conscious and be disciplined. Also, the setup and the environment of this website application will be to mimic banking services. This solves one big problem among teenagers, which is understanding the banking system. We have introduced feature of bonuses in this project; in this feature the parent can list certain jobs/chores that they want their child to perform(household chores, grocery shopping etc.) and if the child performs those tasks, he/she will get some bonus money. Through this feature the child will realize the worth of doing extra work and that it never goes to waste.
